Enjoy Your Dishes!!


"Delight in Every Bite: Where Flavor Meets Tradition!"

Dosa

Dosa is a nutrient-rich, low-fat South Indian dish made from fermented rice and lentil batter, offering benefits like improved digestion, sustained energy, and being naturally gluten-free.

Full-Meals

A traditional South Indian full meal, often referred to as a "South Indian thali," is a wholesome and balanced meal that offers a variety of flavors, textures, and nutritional benefits

Briyani

Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic South Asian dish made with spiced rice, marinated meat or vegetables, and a blend of herbs and spices, offering a rich and satisfying meal.

Mandi-Briyani

Mandi biryani is a traditional Arabian dish featuring spiced rice cooked with marinated meat, typically lamb or chicken, known for its smoky flavor and fragrant spices.

Mutton-Chukka

Mutton chukka is a spicy South Indian dry curry made with tender mutton pieces, aromatic spices, and curry leaves, offering a rich and flavorful experience.

Chicken-Tikka

Chicken tikka is a popular Indian dish consisting of marinated chicken pieces grilled or roasted to perfection, known for its smoky flavor and vibrant spices.

Kothu-parotta

Kothu parotta is a flavorful South Indian street food made by stir-frying shredded parotta (layered flatbread) with eggs, vegetables, and spices, often accompanied by a spicy curry. Shredded parotta, eggs, veggies..

Salad

A salad is a versatile dish typically made from a mixture of fresh vegetables, fruits, and sometimes proteins like chicken or beans, often dressed with a variety of dressings for a light and nutritious meal.

Drinks

Drinks range from revitalizing teas and freshly squeezed juices to indulgent cocktails and invigorating coffees, catering to various tastes and occasions with a spectrum of flavors and effects.
